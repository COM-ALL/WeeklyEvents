APIDA in Aggieland: Telling Our Stories

Monday, April 19, 2021

6:00pm

In honor of Asian, Pacific Islander, and Desi American (APIDA) Heritage Month, the University Libraries in collaboration with the Asian Presidents’ Council offers a safe space for an APIDA student panel to express their views on current events including the increased violence and racism towards the APIDA community since the start of the pandemic, while bringing awareness to the effects of microaggressions and overt racism. The panel intends to start a conversation that evolves into action for allies and the campus, and welcomes the audience to join.
