Military Medicine Series: Law of Armed Conflict: Healthcare & Ethics in a Deployed Environment

Monday, April 12, 2021

12:00pm - 1:00pm

Speaker: Lt Gen (USAF ret) Douglas J. Robb, DO, MPH This presentation is an interactive dialogue that challenges participants to explore ethical challenges facing military medics in armed conflict. The Dual Agency Role of a military medic, namely “are you a medic first or military first?” will be addressed. Scenarios describing medical ethical dilemmas will be reviewed to highlight the challenges facing medics in a deployed environment.
