A Billion Hours of Good

Tuesday, June 8, 2021

9:30am

Join author Chris Field for an inspirational and actionable keynote on how to leverage the talents and skills we already have to unlock massive transformation in our communities and beyond. Among other things, Chris is the founder of Mercy Project, an innovative non–profit that has used economic development and community partnership to rescue nearly 200 children from child slavery in Ghana. This 14–minute keynote, exactly 1% of our day, mirrors the amount of time Chris will show us how to save each day and then turn into meaningful good that will collectively equal a total of a billion hours. We’re each just 14 minutes away from more impact than we’ve ever believed possible.

A PI Protocol: Methods for finding a tenure track position

Thursday, June 10, 2021

12:00pm - 1:00pm

Medical Research Education Building #2

Please join the Office of Postdoctoral Affairs for an upcoming seminar entitled A PI Protocol: Methods for finding a tenure track position presented by Dr. Samantha Bell, postdoctoral research associate in Microbial Pathogenesis & Immunology in the College of Medicine. Dr. Bell will provide an overview of how she recently secured an assistant professor position at Rutgers New Jersey Medical School. She will highlight the strategies she used in her search; the resources she relied on throughout the process; and the lessons she learned along the way.

If you prefer to watch on Zoom, please register, and the link will be sent to you.
